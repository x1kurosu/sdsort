This is a sorter for units in the video game series SD Gundam G Generation.

The content here currently is from SD Gundam G Generation Genesis on PS Vita/PS4/Switch.

There are currently 427 units in this sorter.



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.



Pretty much everything here is based off of the Touhou Project Sorter, source here.
